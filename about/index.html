Following on from a correspondence course in herbal medicine, I decided to train as a professional homeopath and joined the Society of Homeopath’s professional register in the 1990’s, and began to practice.

I also took part in a 2-year course with workshops in transpersonal psychology around the same time, and this training nourished me personally and helped me to understand more about myself and other people.

Transpersonal psychology, Buddhism and 5 rhythms dance have influenced my work in complementary medicine, and my own personal development.

From 2011 I trained in Reiki healing, then as a Reflexologist, as I wished to work in a more hands-on way with people.

More recently I have increased my focus on these therapies and have been amazed at the results.

I now offer:

  • Foot reflexology;
  • Facial reflexology;
  • Homeopathy;
  • Facial reflexology with Reiki healing;
  • Facial reflexology with facelift massage;
  • Crystals with reflexology;
  • And: a combination of facial and foot reflexology in one session.

Also offering specialised home visits for the elderly and/or disabled
(available in Headington or in the OX1/OX2 areas of Oxford)
